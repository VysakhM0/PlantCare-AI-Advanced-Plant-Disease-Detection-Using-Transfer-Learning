Our Mission

Plant diseases cause massive agricultural losses globally every year. Our mission is to democratize access to expert-level agricultural knowledge through the power of AI.

By empowering farmers and hobbyists with instant plant diagnosis, we aim to reduce pesticide use, improve crop yield, and promote sustainable farming practices across the world.
